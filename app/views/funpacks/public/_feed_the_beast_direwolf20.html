Feed The Beast is a modpack for Minecraft. It adds a huge number of items, recipes and world-generation to the game. The modpack centers on several famous mods such as IndustrialCraft and BuildCraft but the full list is extensive.

How to get started

Create your Feed The Beast server on Minefold and then head over to http://feed-the-beast.com/ to download the Client.
